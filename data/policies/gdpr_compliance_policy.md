# GDPR Compliance Policy

## 1. Purpose

This policy governs the organisation's obligations under the UK/EU General
Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for personal data of EU and UK data
subjects.

## 2. Lawful Basis

Processing of personal data must have a documented lawful basis (consent,
contract necessity, legal obligation, vital interest, public task, or
legitimate interest) before processing begins.

## 3. No Exceptions for Individual Clients or Deals

GDPR obligations apply uniformly to all EU/UK data subjects and **may not
be waived, suspended, or ignored for an individual client, deal, or
business relationship**, irrespective of internal approval, client
request, or managerial instruction. Any request to bypass GDPR
requirements for a specific case must be declined and escalated to the
Data Protection Officer (DPO).

## 4. Data Subject Rights

Data subjects retain the right to access, rectify, erase, and port their
data at all times; these rights cannot be contractually waived, including
for high-value or strategic clients.

## 5. Ownership

The Data Protection Officer (DPO) owns this policy and must be notified
of any proposal to depart from it.
